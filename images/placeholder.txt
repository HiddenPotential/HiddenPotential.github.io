This folder is for your images.

You can add:
1. A profile photo as "profile.jpg" (preferably square, will be displayed as a circle)
2. A Roblox screenshot as "roblox.jpg" 
3. A golf photo as "golf.jpg"

Image tips:
- Profile photo should be a close-up of your face or upper body
- Roblox screenshot should show your avatar in your favorite game
- Golf photo should show you playing golf or with your trophies
- Try to use clear, high-quality images with good lighting

Rename your images to something like "roblox.jpg" and "golf.jpg" for easy reference.
